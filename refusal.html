WHAT INSPIRED TARSYS?

I

Internet access is a fundamental human right

TARSYS arose from my personal feelings of loathing when it came to the control surrounding the distribution and access of information.

Especially when it regarded those who controlled internet access and those who were stripped of their right to access the internet and its information.

II

It truly pains me to see these situations where people are denied their information. I refuse to accept any such situations and sought to resolve them.

These situations are unacceptable and exist because people are not in control of the systems that distribute their information.

III

Thus, TARSYS is a reclamation of this right, our right, to distribute digital information through our own means.
